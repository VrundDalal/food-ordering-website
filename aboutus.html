About Us

Our Story

I have always loved food, but I hated the hassle of ordering it online. I was tired of paying high fees, waiting for long delivery times, and getting cold or wrong orders. I wanted a better way to enjoy my favorite dishes from local restaurants and cafes. That’s why I decided to create foodio, a food ordering website that puts customers first. I started by researching the best website builders and platforms to make a food ordering website. I found out that I could use WordPress or a website builder like Orderable1 or Cleveroad2 to create a website for my restaurant. I chose Orderable because it was easy to use, had a lot of features, and didn’t charge any commissions. I then chose a domain name and hosting service for my website. I wanted a name that was catchy, memorable, and related to food. I came up with foodio, which sounded like a combination of food and audio. I registered the domain name foodio.com and signed up for a hosting plan that suited my needs and budget. Next, I designed my website using Orderable’s templates and customization options. I wanted my website to look professional, attractive, and user-friendly. I chose a color scheme that matched my logo, added high-quality photos of my food, and wrote engaging content for my homepage, menu, and about us page. I also added a contact form, a blog, and a newsletter sign-up to connect with my customers. After designing my website, I customized the order placing process using Orderable’s features. I wanted to offer my customers the option to choose between local pickup and delivery, select their own delivery time and date, and pay online using various methods. I also wanted to provide them with real-time order tracking, customer support, and loyalty rewards. I set up all these features using Orderable’s dashboard and plugins. THIS IS MY STORY
